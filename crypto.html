What is Cryptocurrency


Cryptocurrency (also known as crypto) is a form of payment/ currency that are not regulated by the bank and government. Cryptocurrency has been around since 2009 with the creation of Bitcoin and now, there is over 16000 different cryptocurrencies on the market, but the 2 with the biggest market capitilastion are Bitcoin and Ethereum. At the moment, cryptocurrencies are mostly used as an investment, but they can also be used in different transactions to acquire products and other things.

Crypto investing and risks involved

The first thing you need if you want to buy and use your cryptocurrency is to created an account on something called a "cryptocurrency wallet" which is a software that can be installed on your computer or phone and like a wallet it contains your currency. Once you have your cryptocurrency wallet set up, you can now begin to purchase crypto currencies. Some cryptocurrencies, like Bitcoin are available for purchase with real money while others you need to buy it with other forms of cryptocurrency. Crptocurrency can benefit you greatly if things go right with your investment but there are also risks that come with investing in them. The market for cryptocurrency fluctuates and it's worth can go up very fast like Bitcoin but it can also drop very quickly.So if you bought your cryptocurrency at it's peak and it drops in value, you can suffer major losses on your investment. They are also not regulated by banks or the government so they won't be insured. Finally cryptocurrencies are digital assets so they can be hacked and stolen from you which is another risk thay can come with investng in crypto.
