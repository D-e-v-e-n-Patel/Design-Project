NFT's and Crypto

This is an informative website about the Nft and Crypto space which are relitavely new forms of currency/assets. Crypto currency has been around since Bitcoin was made in 2009 and Nft's have been around since 2014 when Kevin McCoy, a digital artist, minted "Quantum"which is the first ever known Nft. To learn more about them continue reading to the introduction about each one of them or use the navigation bar to learn more in depth about both of these topics.

Cryptocurrency (simple defintion)

Crytpo currency is a form of currency that replaces the tradition currency of paper bills and metal coins by using digital files as currency.

Nft's (simple defintion)

"NFT" is an acronym for "Non Fundgible token" which are unique digital tokens/assets that cannot be reproduced since it uses blockchain technology that keeps a digital record of that NFT.
